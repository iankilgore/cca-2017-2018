22. Esker - are made of sand and rocks that are left by streams under the glaciers created while the glacier is melting.
moraine - there are several kinds of moraines such as end moraines and latteral moraines. Basically when glaciers melt they leave piles of till showing where they once were. Lateral moraines form on the side of glaciers, and an end moraine shows the farthest extent of it before it melted away.
kettle - Sometimes a glacier melts leaving a chunk of ice in the ground. When it melts, it fills in the space with a lake.
drumlin - drumlins are basically hills made up of till.
